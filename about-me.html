Favorite childhood memories

I lived all of my childhood in my home country Colombia, some of the best memories I enjoy to remember are about sleepovers. My entire family is there and we used to be very close so my aunts would let my counsins and I have a lot of them, it would usually start with us going to the park and play lots of games like hide and seek and tag, and then when it began to be too late we would go back home to play even more inside, watch movies, play with my wii and stay up until the last one fell asleep, which is the one who gets to prank the rest (I was usually the one who fell asleep last) and we would draw doodles in everyones faces without waking them up. My childhood school also made a sleepover one time and I enjoyed it a lot with my friends, which I still talk to to this day.
